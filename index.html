What changed in Studio

Feature Use
Program timeline Chain multiple layer stacks into one session: warm-up, drill, recovery, sleep descent, or experiments.
Micro-drift Add tiny beat movement so long sessions feel less rigid. Set to 0% for exact frequency tests.
Start / stop offsets Stagger layers, fade in a noise bed first, or stop a high-frequency layer before the wind-down.
URL share Encode the whole current setup into the page hash for easy handoff without a server.
Pan motion Move any non-binaural layer through the stereo field with a slow sine LFO while keeping binaural ear separation intact.
Ambience files Load local rain, bowls, field recordings, or other audio as looped layers; they render into WAV exports while the buffer remains in browser RAM only.
