Multi-wave audio lab

ENTRAIN+

A safer, more flexible tone console for attention practice: stack several isochronic, binaural, monaural, carrier, and noise layers; pan them; ramp them; save presets; and export the full mix as a WAV.

Build a layered session.

This version is built around combinations. Each layer has its own method, beat rate, carrier, volume, pan, ramp, mute, and solo state. A master limiter helps prevent clipping when layers are stacked.

Volume safety: keep the master low at first. Stacked waves can sound louder than one layer even when each layer is quiet. Do not use while driving, and avoid pulsing audio/visual stimuli if you have a seizure history unless a clinician clears it.
